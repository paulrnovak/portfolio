The Dollop Timeline

Web Design
Web Development

This fun little site is an homage to my favorite podcast, The Dollop. It's a bi-weekly american history podcast. Each week they analyze a topic from American history, at the beginning of each episode the host Dave yells the date of said topic.

Since the topics jump all around the history books I wanted a way to see the episodes in order of the dates of each topic. While also wanting to learn how to use Webflow's symbols and interactions better, I made the site myself.

(This is an unsolicited personal project purely made for fun and to learn new techniques – this is also very much a work in progress and ongoing.)
