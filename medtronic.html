The primary part of my job since late 2019 has been working with one of Halftone’s main clients, Medtronic, as a product designer. Starting with some simple design & prototyping work, I helped foster and grow our working relationship with Medtronic into a multi-year, ongoing, and fully integrated design partnership.

I have spent the majority of this time designing mobile applications for clinicians and patients in their portfolio of neuromodulation therapies. I have spent time developing and defining their design language and systems across various therapy and device types, as well as working from the infancy of products, utilizing user flows, wireframes, and prototyping to test and validate direction and help define scope.

The nature of this work is confidential. I would be happy to walk you through some of this work — feel free to send me an email to set up a meeting.
