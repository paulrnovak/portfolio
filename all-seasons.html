All Seasons Preschool

Web Design
Web Development

I've known Sarah, the director of All Seasons Preschool, since high school, so I was more than happy to get the call to help her out and, more importantly, help out a local preschool with a website refresh. Their old site had lots of great content and information, but lacked the proper visual representation of the school and its ethos.

I built a custom Squarespace template for them with a fully refreshed color and type direction, along with a fully overhauled navigation and information architecture. Working with the existing info and content, much of that was ported over and kept alive in this new space.
