MGK.com

Web Design

One of my first projects during my time at Rocket55 was to help the 100+ year-old company MGK® redefine their website presence. Coming to us with a site from the early 2000s, we had nowhere to go but up. Leveraging modern web technologies and a large host of all-new content to work from the site took form quickly and we were off and running.

We gave them a large framework to build upon, including things like a full pest and resource library, robust product filtering, a dilution calculator, and a blog to help showcase their leadership within their field.
