Six Dot Domino Club

Branding

Six Dot is a place for people to gather and talk slick all over a game of dominoes. In its infancy still, working as a series of pop-up events with the goal in mind to grow into its very own space. Knowing how great the people behind the idea are, I was more than willing to give them my assistance is building out the concept.

I collaborated with the crew on the logo and identity, as well as print materials that help newcomers visualize the game and promote the events.
