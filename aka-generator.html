The brothers of ItsTheReal made one of my favorite podcasts and do some of the best interviews in music. If you know them then you know the signature AKA's they shout at the top of every episode. Being a fan and wanting to learn some new stuff in Webflow I decided to try and make a fun little microsite that archives these hilarious lines.

(This is an unsolicited personal project purely made for fun and to learn new techniques – this is also very much a work in progress and ongoing.)
