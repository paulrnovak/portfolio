DMJ Jewelry

Branding

My buddy Dan has been making art and jewelry long before I ever met him. From the first time we met I instantly clicked with his personality and art style and knew if given the chance I would love to design some stuff and help him get a web shop going. Using the fun motifs and reoccuring themes in Dan’s work, ideas were sprouting before we even sat down together.

His mix of bright colors, heavy graffiti influence and his knack for turning objects into characters were the perfect places to work from. Creating a little type and illustration system to reflect the ideas in Dan’s head was very fun and I’m looking forward to expanding on it.
