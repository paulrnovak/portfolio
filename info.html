I’m Paul Novak a designer living and working in Minneapolis, MN.

Currently I'm working as a Creative Director at Halftone Digital a small but mighty remote agency specializing in product design & animation. My work there primarily consists of designing applications, products & systems for medical devices with Medtronic's Neuromodulation team.

Away from my day job I run a teeny freelance business, building and managing websites for a few clients a year and designing logos and ephemera for friends working on cool things. I also teach web design at the University of Minnesota on a part time basis a couple of days a week.

My design work reflects my influences in simple and intuitive interfaces as well as clean and engaging brands and identities. My favorite types of projects are when I get to work with great people and learn new skills.
